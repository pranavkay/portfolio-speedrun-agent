Questions

Do I need to know how to code?

No. The AI assistant handles all the code. You just answer questions about your work, pick colors, and review the result.

How much does it cost?

The template is free and open source. Hosting on Vercel is free. The only cost is your AI assistant subscription (Claude, Codex, or Cursor).

Can I change things after I deploy?

Yes. Your site has a built-in admin panel at /admin where you can update everything — portfolio items, bio, photos, testimonials — without touching code.

What if I want a custom design?

Start with a preset and customize from there. You can change colors, fonts, sections, and layout. For advanced customization, the code is clean and well-organized.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. Vercel lets you connect a custom domain for free. Just buy a domain from any registrar and point it to your Vercel project.
